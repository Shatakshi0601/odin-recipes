Batti

Description

Batti, often associated with traditional Rajasthani cuisine, typically refers to a type of baked bread or a dish that might be served with dal (lentils).

Ingretients

  • Whole Wheat Flour (Atta) – 2 cups
  • Semolina (Sooji) – 2 tablespoons (optional, for texture)
  • Baking Powder – 1 teaspoon Salt – 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter) – 2 tablespoons (for mixing into the dough)
  • Water – As needed to make the dough
  • Caraway Seeds (Ajwain) – 1/2 teaspoon (optional, for added flavor)
  • Cumin Seeds (Jeera) – 1/2 teaspoon (optional, for added flavor)
